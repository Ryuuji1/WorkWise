Instructions

Step 1: Resume

Review your resume to ensure the veracity and accuracy of all information presented.

Step 2: Cover Letter

Compose a cover letter aligned with your resume and the position you're applying for.

Step 3: Send Email

Compose an email with the subject line [Your last, first name] | Applicant | [Position] then make your cover letter as the body of your email and send it to recruitmentph@workwise.com with your resume attached.

Step 4: Confirmation

Please check your inbox and spam folder for a confirmation email. After you confirm, you should hear from us within 3 to 5 business days.

Optional: Multiple Entries

You may send multiple applications for different positions by repeating the steps above for each application. Just make sure that the cover letter and resume correspond to the specific job you are applying for. Good luck!
